ANVIL


NUB is the smallest robot yet. I was inspired by a super small container (Shown Below). The first container was too small to work with, so I went one side up. This robot is designed for remote operation, it has 2 high powered and compact motors (actually faster than Paper Weight.) NUB also has a IP camera that connects directly to a cell phone for viewing. It has a compass, barometer and temperature sensor. It is water resistant as well. By far the smallest robot I have made yet
