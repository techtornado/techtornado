Robot 1 (The very first robot!)


Robot 1 was the very first robot I created. I got the ultrasonic sensor (the eyes) from radio shack and I am pretty sure that the motors came from a hobby store. The robot its self was part of a project enclosure also from radio shack. It was very simple, just the Arduino, Servos, and ultrasonic sensor. The wheels were also made of CDs which was the only round thing I could find.


In this next video you can see just how terrible this robot is. It has very limited AI and very bad turning mechanics.
