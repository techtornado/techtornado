Robot 10


Robot 10 was the first bot to be a 1' by 1' square. It used the same motors as robot 5 and the same kind of batteries. This robot had autonomous as well as remote control. The radio was a Xbee 2.4 ghz wireless radio. I then used a laptop to drive it. This robot also had a voice recorder that was triggered from a servo physically pressing the button. Not very mechanically sound but that is also I knew how to do at the time. I also used more LEDs on it and it also had a LCD and buttons with a simple menu
