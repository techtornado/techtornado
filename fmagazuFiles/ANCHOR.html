ANCHOR (first version)


Okay, so this was ANCHOR. ANCHOR which stands for (Advanced Numerically controlled Hybrid Operating Robot ) He was what robot 16 was suppose to be. I worked on him on and off for 2 years. He was by far the most complex robot yet (robot 16 and before) He had a ton of sensors, the same motors as robot 16 and a whole windows XP computer inside of him complete with computer monitor. He also had a ice tea dispenser. A full GUI was created on the computer that allowed full control of it. The software was also the most advanced GUI I made for any robot.
