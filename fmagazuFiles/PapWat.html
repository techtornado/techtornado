Paper Weight


This is Paper Weight, No clever acronym though. It is just lighter than ANCHOR so Paper Weight seems appropriate. Paper Weight has remote control and autonomous modes . It has a IP Camera and many sensors. It also has a 1 axis arm that it can use to sense its environment by using the IR sensor and switch on the end of the arm.
